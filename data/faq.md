# RobotZio FAQ

## What is RobotZio?
RobotZio is a robotics marketplace platform for the Middle East. Buyers can discover robotics products, solutions, services, rentals, and parts. Merchants can list and promote their offerings.

## Does RobotZio sell robots directly?
No. RobotZio is a marketplace platform, not a direct robot seller.

## How can buyers request pricing?
Buyers can submit RFQ (Request for Quote) forms from the homepage Smart RFQ section or from product listing/detail workflows.

## Can merchants join the platform?
Yes. Merchants can apply via the Join as Merchant page and submit company profile, product categories, and catalog details.

## What is the early-stage business model?
The early model includes merchant listing fees, featured placement fees, and premium exposure positions on homepage and product pages.

## Which categories are core on the homepage?
The homepage highlights featured products and core categories such as robot parts, kits, rentals, education solutions, service robots, industrial solutions, after-sales and repair, robot training, and data collection.

## Which regions are supported?
The platform focuses on the Middle East and GCC, including UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain, and Oman.

## How is support handled?
Users can submit technical or sales support requests from the Support page. Merchant and partnership inquiries are also handled through dedicated forms.
