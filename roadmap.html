RobotZio 90-Day Launch Roadmap

Execution plan for building the first robotics marketplace layer in the Middle East with merchant revenue validation in 3 months.

Month 1 - Build Supply Density

  • Onboard first 20-30 verified merchants across UAE and Saudi Arabia
  • Launch focused categories: Hospitality, Security, Logistics
  • Publish merchant listing plans and exposure packages
  • Release merchant onboarding and listing submission workflow

Month 2 - Launch Buyer RFQ Engine

  • Release Smart RFQ form for structured buyer demand capture
  • Build merchant inquiry inbox and response workflow
  • Add qualification fields: budget, timeline, region, use case
  • Track lead quality metrics and merchant response SLA

Month 3 - Validate Revenue

  • Close first paid listing and featured placement deals
  • Optimize buyer conversion from listing view to RFQ submit
  • Define qualified lead criteria for future lead-based billing
  • Publish performance dashboard for merchant retention

North-Star Metrics (First 90 Days)

Merchant Activation

Verified merchants with complete profiles and published listings.

RFQ Conversion Rate

Product/solution visits converted into Smart RFQ submissions.

Qualified Lead Ratio

Leads matching budget, timeline, and decision-maker criteria.

Merchant Response SLA

Average first-response time and quote completion ratio.
